Let'sHelp

The Helping Hands Foundation is a voluntary organization and is always in need of your help. While we have our trustees and sponsors, we still need volunteers to help us execute our plans. If you would like to dedicate some of your time to helping others, feel free to get in touch with us.

THE SURVIVOR PROJECT

The Survivor Project aims towards regular testing post recovery, ensuring minimum relapses, continued education and ensuring a smoother transition into mainstream society.

HEY THERE

Only 2 of 10 children survive Cancer in India compared to 8 out of 10 children worldwide. We are striving to improve this and also take it further by helping the children become productive citizens in society post treatment.
